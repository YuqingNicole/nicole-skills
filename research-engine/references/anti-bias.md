# Anti-Bias & Evidence Framework

## Evidence Tiers

| Tier | Label | Sources | Trust level |
|------|-------|---------|-------------|
| T1 | Primary | SEC filings, official stats, your own data, direct interviews | High — use as foundation |
| T2 | Factual secondary | Crunchbase, reputable press, academic papers, verified market reports | Medium — corroborate when possible |
| T3 | Opinion/inference | Analyst commentary, social media, AI synthesis, forum discussion | Low — context only, never sole basis for recommendation |

**Labeling rule:** In reports, tag each key claim: `[T1]`, `[T2]`, `[T3]`.

## Common Biases in Market Research

### Confirmation Bias
You searched for evidence that supports the thesis.  
**Counter:** Actively search for the best argument *against*. Write it out.

### Anchoring
Your TAM estimate is suspiciously close to the first number you found.  
**Counter:** Derive from multiple methods (top-down + bottom-up). Compare.

### Availability Bias  
You're overweighting a dramatic recent event (funding round, product launch, viral post).  
**Counter:** Check base rates. What's the historical rate of success in this category?

### Survivorship Bias  
You researched the successful competitors, not the ones that failed.  
**Counter:** Ask: what did the failed companies in this space look like? Why did they fail?

### Narrative Fallacy
A compelling story replaced rigorous data.  
**Counter:** Does the data actually support the story, or did you construct the story to explain the data?

## Pre-Mortem Template

Before finalizing any strategic recommendation:

> "It's 12 months from now. The strategy failed. What are the 3 most likely reasons?"

1. [Most likely reason — be specific, not "market conditions"]
2. [Second reason]
3. [Third reason]

Then: for each reason, does the strategy have a response? If not — either de-risk or acknowledge the assumption explicitly.

## Quick Anti-Bias Checklist

- [ ] Did I search for contradicting evidence?
- [ ] Did I cross-check the TAM with at least 2 methods?
- [ ] Did I account for failed competitors, not just successful ones?
- [ ] Is my primary thesis resting on T1/T2 evidence? (Not T3 alone)
- [ ] Pre-mortem completed and documented?
- [ ] Are all key numbers labeled with their evidence tier?
